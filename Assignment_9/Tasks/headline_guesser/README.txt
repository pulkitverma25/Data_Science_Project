TASK: Given the name of a newspaper, guess the headline that appeared on the frontpage.

DESCRIPTION. We will now ask you 20 multiple-choice questions. For each question, we will give you name of the newspaper, and ask you to guess the headline which had appeared on frontpage in the given newspaper.

CORPUS: Names of newspapers and their headlines were chosen from:
	http://www.thepaperboy.com/india/front-pages.cfm
	
		
METHODOLOGY: 
	TASKS
	A random sample of 20 headlines and the corresponding newspaper are chosen. Name of the newspaper is displayed as input text and corresponding headline is recorded as the correct answer.
	
	ANSWERS
	For each task, four random samples of headlines that dont belong to the given newspaper are selected.
